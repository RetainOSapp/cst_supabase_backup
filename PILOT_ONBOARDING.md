# RetainOS Ethical Scaling Pilot Onboarding

Use this guide as a short onboarding-video script for Ben and Emily.

## Pilot Goal

The goal is to use RetainOS during normal Ethical Scaling client-success work, confirm the essential workflows feel reliable, and identify improvements before migrating additional companies.

RetainOS is available at:

`https://app.retainos.ai`

## 1. Sign In

1. Open `https://app.retainos.ai`.
2. Enter your approved work email.
3. Click **Sign In**.
4. Open the RetainOS email sent from `support@ethicalscaling.com`.
5. Enter the login code.

Your role and Ethical Scaling access are determined automatically.

## 2. Pilot Access

### Ben: Director

- Can view all Ethical Scaling clients.
- Can create and update clients.
- Can assign or reassign clients.
- Can update outcomes, milestones, contracts, and statuses.
- Can manage Ethical Scaling team members.

### Emily: CSM

- Can view assigned Ethical Scaling clients only.
- Can use Quick Update on assigned clients.
- Can update outcomes and milestone progress for assigned clients.
- Can add contracts and change statuses for assigned clients.
- Cannot reassign clients or change company pathways.

Neither role should see another company's data.

## 3. Everyday Workflow

### Start From Clients

Use the **Clients** page for normal client-success work.

- Filters and the selected view persist when opening a client and returning.
- Use List, Cards, or Calendar depending on the task.
- The reminder section highlights upcoming or recently overdue next contacts, renewals, and paused-client return dates.

### Quick Update

Use **Quick Update** for regular CSM updates:

- Next Steps
- Notes
- Date of Last Contact
- Date of Next Contact
- Success
- Progress
- Buy-in
- Complete Current Milestone

Completing a milestone automatically advances the client to the next configured milestone. Changing the client's full pathway remains on the Client Detail page.

### Client Detail

Open a client for deeper work:

- Edit profile and North Star
- Review or create contracts
- Review program/status
- Update outcomes
- Review and manage pathway milestones
- Review client tasks
- Review History

History should show important RetainOS changes, including Quick Updates, outcomes, contracts, statuses, assignments, and milestones.

### New Client

Use **+ New Client** to create a client.

During creation, you can optionally configure:

- Primary CSM
- Initial status
- Offer/pathway
- Starting milestone
- Initial contract dates

## 4. Pilot Source-of-Truth Rule

For workflows supported in RetainOS, make the update in **RetainOS only**.

Do not repeat the same update in Glide during the pilot. This prevents conflicting records and lets us validate RetainOS properly.

Use Glide only when a required workflow is not yet available in RetainOS.

## 5. What To Validate

During normal work, confirm:

- You see the correct clients for your role.
- Client data looks accurate.
- Quick Updates save and appear in History.
- Milestone completion advances correctly.
- Outcomes, contracts, assignments, and status changes save correctly.
- Calendar dates and reminders look useful.
- Filters remain selected when returning from a client.
- You never see another company's data.

## 6. Reporting Feedback

When reporting an issue, include:

1. The client name.
2. The page and action attempted.
3. What you expected.
4. What happened instead.
5. A screenshot when possible.
6. Whether the issue blocks your work or is only an improvement request.

Avoid testing destructive actions on active clients. Use an offboarded or clearly temporary client when possible.

## 7. Pilot Success Criteria

The internal pilot is successful when:

- Ben and Emily can complete normal client-success work without returning to Glide for supported workflows.
- Role permissions and company isolation remain correct.
- RetainOS data stays accurate throughout the pilot.
- Blocking issues are resolved before migrating another company.
